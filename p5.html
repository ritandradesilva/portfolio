shoe lé

branding and sneaker customization,
porto 2020.

Shoe Lé was a sneaker customization project. It was a collaboration with a friend (Eduardo Rothes), where we took our common interest of sneakers and added my artistic abilities and his social skills.

Working with each client, I created a different design for every custom, and it could go from recreations of iconic works/characters, to original drawings of mine. All paintings were made by me, as well as the branding (logo, illustrations, stickers).
