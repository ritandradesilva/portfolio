diving in

album cover and merch,
vienna 2024.

Diving In is the latest EP by the indie viennese band Painting Clouds and I designed the album and some merchandise for the release show that took place on February 20 at the Kramladen in Vienna.

There are two different oil pastel covers that can be swapped by flipping the booklet over, as well as some other elements that were used for the t-shirts, stickers, postcards and virtual posters.
